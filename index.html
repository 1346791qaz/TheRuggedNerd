The Rugged Nerd Crew

I'm The Rugged Nerd — a tech enthusiast who swapped the office for open roads. Together with my amazing wife, our three wild kids, and our two loyal dogs, we load up our trusty truck Blue and our 25-foot travel trailer Dorey to explore everything from mountain switchbacks to coastal hideaways.

This site is our field manual: real trip reports, hard-won RV tips, honest gear reviews, and all the downloads you need to make your own adventures less chaotic and more legendary.
