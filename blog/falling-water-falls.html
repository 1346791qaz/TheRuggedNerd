Falling Water Falls

🌊

Falling Water Falls in Sand Gap, Arkansas, is one of those spots that rewards a little extra effort to get off the main route. The original copy of this post calls it a hidden gem, and that description still fits.

Trip Snapshot

Falling Water Falls, Sand Gap, Arkansas. This hidden gem is off the beaten path and an absolutely beautiful site. Located just minutes from Pedestal Rock and King's Bluff Trails in Sand Gap, Arkansas, the falls provide a stunning aural and visual experience...

Map Link from the Original Post

https://maps.app.goo.gl/MN6JmmU5pJrUiGSa7

Why We Like This Stop

It is a strong add-on to a North Arkansas weekend loop: big scenery, trail options nearby, and a quick way to make a short trip feel like a real adventure.
